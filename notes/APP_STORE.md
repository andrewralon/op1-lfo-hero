# App Store Metadata

## Description

op1 lfo hero turns your iPhone into a beat-synced LFO controller for the Teenage Engineering OP-1 Field — adding rhythmic, expressive automation to your tracks with no extra hardware.

Connect via USB-C or Bluetooth, set a waveform and rate, and any parameter on your OP-1 Field starts moving in time with your music: volume swells, panning motion, mute rhythms, FX sweeps, and more.

FEATURES

· 9 LFO waveforms: sine, triangle, square, sawtooth, ramp, and more
· Per-track control for all 4 tracks — volume, pan, mute, FX 1–4, LFO 1–4
· Master bus control — master FX 1–4 and compressor 1–4
· Beat-synced rates from 1/32 note to 32 bars
· Loop, one-shot, and inverted modes
· Live waveform preview — see the curve before it hits the OP-1
· MIDI clock master: app generates tempo and drives the OP-1
· MIDI clock slave: OP-1 leads, app follows
· USB-C and Bluetooth LE MIDI
· Keeps running in the background — screen can lock, LFOs keep going

REQUIREMENTS

· Teenage Engineering OP-1 Field (not compatible with the original OP-1)
· iPhone running iOS 16 or later
