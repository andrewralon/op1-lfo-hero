# App Store Metadata

## Description

the op-1 field is perfect. almost.

op1 lfo hero connects to your op1 and uses external midi magic to create custom lfos to modulate parameters to the beat of your music. pick a thing to automate — volume, pan, mute, fx, and more! — set a waveform and a speed, and let your ears enjoy. make music fun.

that's about it. it's mildly fantastic, just like your face.

stuff it does:
· lfo parameters for all tracks and the master bus - who knew?
· plus a few things the op1 can't control on its own ....
· did you know you can make an lfo for tempo? no you do
· yes, you can even create lfos for lfos
· a satisfying number of waveforms
· rates synced to the beat, from fast to glacially slow [thanks AI]
· loop, one-shot, invert — the usual options [also AI]
· tempo modes: beat sync (op1 master) and midi sync (app master) — you decide who's the boss [you guessed it]
· runs in the background, screen locked, like a boss

stuff it doesn't do:
· include an op-1 field - consider it a $2000 upgrade to this app
· explain the magic of midi or what "lfo" means
· pick the waveform for you — choose your own adventure, bruh
· fix your timing or inability to carry a tune
· record audio, generate melodies, or do anything else remotely useful

requirements:
· teenage engineering op-1 field - incompatible with the original, as far as i know
· iphone - ios 16 or later
· connect via usb-c or bluetooth ble midi
