op1 lfo hero

make music fun - custom midi lfos for the op-1 field by teenage engineering

connection

  • connect iphone to op1 via usb-c or bluetooth (ble midi)
  • open the app — automatically connects to op1
  • status bar shows op-1 field (usb) or op-1 field (ble)

tempo modes

  • app (midi sync) — app is master. app controls basic op1 tape transport (play/stop/back/forward).
    • tempo lfo requires usb-c. bluetooth doesn't send high-resolution tempo changes.
  • op1 (beat match) — op1 is master. app syncs lfos to op1's tempo.

toggle the app's tempo mode with the metronome button in the transport column, then change the op1 to match.
