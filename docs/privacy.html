op1 lfo hero

privacy policy  ·  effective june 16, 2026

no data collected

op1 lfo hero does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. no account is required. no analytics or tracking of any kind is present in the app.

bluetooth

bluetooth is used solely to discover and connect to a teenage engineering op-1 field. no data is sent to any server. all communication stays between your device and the op1.

midi

midi messages generated by the app go directly to the connected op1 over usb or bluetooth. nothing is logged, stored, or sent anywhere else.

third-party services

the app contains no third-party sdks, advertising networks, or analytics libraries.
