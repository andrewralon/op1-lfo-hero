troubleshooting

  • app shows "no device found"
    make sure the op1 is powered on. for usb: try a different cable — some usb-c cables are charge-only and carry no data. for bluetooth: confirm bluetooth: on is set on the op1.
  • controls send but op1 doesn't respond
    unplug and replug the op1, or restart lfo hero. if using bluetooth, toggle bluetooth: off then back on on the op1.
  • tape doesn't respond to play / stop
    confirm the op1 is in midi sync mode and the app is in app clock mode. the op1's BPM display should show -- while waiting for clock.
  • op1 tempo doesn't match the app
    the op1 needs a few seconds of continuous clock to lock in after a tempo change. note: bluetooth cannot handle high-resolution tempo changes (LFO tempo modulation requires usb).
  • left / right tape buttons stop playback
    this is normal op1 behavior — receiving a song position pointer while playing causes it to pause. press play to resume.
